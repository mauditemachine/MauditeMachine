Maudite Machine is a Canadian DJ and producer known for his raw, hypnotic approach to dark disco and indie dance. With over 15 years in the Montreal scene, he has performed at major events and iconic venues across the country, delivering sets that blur the line between intensity and atmosphere. As the founder of VRSTL Records, he curates a sound that embraces tension, groove, and experimentation. His collaborations with influential artists in the electronic music world reflect a constant drive to push boundaries and redefine the underground with a distinct sonic signature.
